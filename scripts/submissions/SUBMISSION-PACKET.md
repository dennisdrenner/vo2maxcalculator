## Recommended order of operations

1. **AlternativeTo first** (DA ~85, takes longest, sets you up for everything else)
2. **Indie Hackers Products** (free, audience-aligned, fast)
3. **SaaSHub** (low friction)
4. **BetaList** (submit + forget — review queue is 1-2 weeks)
5. **ProLinkDirectory, A1WebDirectory, SoMuch** (low DA but free, do them in one batch when you have 15 min)
6. **Product Hunt last** — schedule a launch date 2-3 weeks out, gather assets, line up a couple of friends to upvote on launch morning. Don't burn this one without prep.

## What I think you should NOT spend time on

- Any "300+ free directory submission" lists you find online — they're SEO-spam from the early-2010s era; Google has actively devalued them and several are domains that hurt more than help
- Calculator-aggregator outreach (Omni / Calculator.net / CalculatorSoup / The Calculator Site) — they all have their own VO2 max calcs; they're competitors, not partners
- Awesome Indie / Micro Launch / Launch Today / Toolify / Future Tools / There's An AI — wrong niche or low DA
- IDEA FitnessConnect — looks great on paper, but it's for fitness PROFESSIONALS, not tools

If you want to spend time beyond this packet, write one good guest post on a real fitness blog. That single editorial link will outperform every entry on this list combined.
