# RRCA outreach (Road Runners Club of America)

**Target:** rrca.org — RRCA is the largest US association of running clubs.
They have a member-resources section that occasionally links out to free
running tools and resources. There is no "submit a tool" form; this is a
cold pitch to the right person.

**Where to find a contact:**
- rrca.org/about/contact (general)
- rrca.org/programs (look for member-services or coaching contacts)
- Their newsletter editor — sometimes listed at the bottom of recent newsletters

## Subject line options

- "Free VO2 max calculator for RRCA members — would it be useful?"
- "VO2 max field-test calculator + percentile chart — for the RRCA resource library?"

## Notes

- Keep it short. Don't lead with "free backlink please."
- The CPET-experience and biology-degree credentials matter — they signal
  this isn't a content-marketing dump.
- If they decline or don't reply, don't follow up more than once after 2 weeks.
